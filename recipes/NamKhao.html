Nam Khao

Ingredients


Nam Khao
2.5 cups cooked rice
2 tbsp Thai red curry paste
2 cloves garlic
1 shallot
3 lime leaves
3 tsp sugar
3 tsp light soy sauce
1 Lettuce
Dressing
2 tbsp light soy sauce
1 chili
2 cloves garlic
1/2 lime juice
2tbsp sugar
1/4 cup water

Mix the rice, curry paste, 2 cloves of garlic, shallot, lime leaves, sugar, and soy sauce together into small balls.
Shallow fry until crispy.

Mix the remaining dressing ingredients together, and combine with the rice.

Serve in lettuce wraps.
