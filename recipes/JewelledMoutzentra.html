Jewelled Moutzentra

Ingredients


250g dried green or brown lentils
130g long grain rice
4 onions
3 garlic cloves
4 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 pomegranate
1 bunch flat leaf parsley
50g toasted flaked almonds
50g dried sour cherries
1/2 lemon

Place a large pan of salted water on to boil.
Thoroughly rinse the lentils in a sieve, picking out any stones, and then cook in the boiling water for 20 minutes until almost cooked.

Meanwhile, rinse the rice in a sieve, then add to the pan for a further 12 minutes, until perfectly cooked.
Drain the rice and lentils in a sieve or fine colander, and return to the hot pan to steam dry and keep fluffy.

While the lentils are cooking, peel and finely slice the onions and garlic.
Place a large frying pan on a medium heat and pour in the oil.
Saut√© the onions, garlic, cumin, and a good pinch of salt and pepper for 20 minutes until deep golden brown.
Make sure to stir occasionally.

Meanwhile, pick the pomegranate seeds.
Pick and roughly chop the flat leaf parsley, and toss them with the toasted flaked almonds, sour cherries, and pomegranate seeds.
Squeeze over th juice from the lemon half, and toss it all together.
When the onions are ready, stir through the cooked lentils, check the seasoning, and plate up.
Garnish with the dressed fruits and nuts and serve.
